Gaborone, Botswana Chamber of Commerce

This name represent the city the chamber oprates in. I included Botswana in the name so that someone can already tell that it is from Botswana

Purpose

Gaborone, Botswana Chamber of Commerce's purpose is to ensure that individuals, comapnies and organizations comes together to help develop the cityt and ensure that business grows in the city. The chamber also make sure that people are aware of what is happening around the city and see how they can engage in.
